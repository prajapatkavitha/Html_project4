ChickPea Curry Recipe

Ingredients :

  1. 1 cup Chickpeas (Chole), soaked overnight
  2. 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 medium Tomatoes, pureed
  4. 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  5. 2 Green Chilies, chopped
  6. 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  7. 1 Bay Leaf
  8. 1 Black Cardamom
  9. 2-3 Cloves
  10. 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  11. 2 tablespoons Chole Masala
  12. 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  13. 1 teaspoon Red Chili Powder
  14. 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  15. 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  16. Salt, to taste
  17. 2 tablespoons Oil
  18. Water, as needed
  19. Fresh Coriander Leaves, for garnish

Preparation Process :

  1. Chickpeas: Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with water and
    a pinch of salt until soft (about 4-5 whistles).
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf,
    black cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Sauté for a few seconds.
    Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
    Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and chole masala.
    Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Combine Chickpeas and Masala: Add the cooked chickpeas to the masala.
    Mix well and add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
    Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Finish and Garnish: Add garam masala and adjust salt.
    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
